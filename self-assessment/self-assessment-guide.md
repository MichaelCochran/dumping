# Professional Development Self-Assessment Guide

**Purpose:** This guide is designed for evaluating your professional capabilities in the workplace and career context. Focus on your work-related skills, not personal life attributes.

## Core Assessment Areas

What do you consider your strengths and weaknesses? Be honest with yourself. Think about things like:

- Technical skills / academic subjects
- Interpersonal skills
- Oral and written communication
- Ability/willingness to learn
- Leadership
<!-- - Financial skills (business/budget management - currently not included) -->
- Problem solving ability (ability to size up a situation and figure out where to start / how to approach the problem to solve it)
- Learning habits & professional development (how you approach acquiring new skills, staying current in your field)
- Emotional Intelligence (awareness of others, their needs, their emotional state, their mental state)
- Systems engineering / systems thinking

**What areas do you feel you can / should improve?**

## Additional Self-Assessment Dimensions

### **Adaptability & Change Management**
- How do you handle unexpected changes to plans or priorities?
- Comfort level with ambiguity and uncertainty
- Ability to pivot strategies when circumstances shift
- Response to constructive criticism and feedback

### **Time & Priority Management**
- Ability to meet deadlines consistently
- Skill at estimating task duration
- Capacity to balance multiple competing priorities
- Tendency toward procrastination or early completion

### **Creativity & Innovation**
- Comfort with generating novel ideas vs. following established methods
- Willingness to challenge conventional approaches
- Ability to think laterally across domains

### **Learning Habits & Development**
- Consistency in professional development activities (courses, certifications, conferences)
- Ability to apply new knowledge to work situations
- Keeping skills current with industry trends
- Effectiveness of your approach to learning new tools/methodologies

### **Collaboration & Teamwork**
- Preference for independent vs. collaborative work
- Ability to compromise and find middle ground
- Giving and receiving help effectively
- Handling conflict within teams

### **Attention to Detail vs. Big Picture Thinking**
- Balance between perfectionism and pragmatism
- Catching errors vs. getting stuck in minutiae
- Strategic vision vs. tactical execution

### **Self-Motivation & Initiative**
- Drive without external supervision
- Proactivity in identifying problems/opportunities
- Follow-through on commitments

### **Decision Making**
- Speed vs. thoroughness in making choices
- Comfort with risk-taking
- Ability to make decisions with incomplete information
- Track record of decision quality

### **Cultural & Diversity Awareness**
- Understanding of different perspectives and backgrounds
- Inclusive behavior and language
- Openness to unfamiliar ideas and practices

### **Technical Depth vs. Breadth**
- Specialist expertise vs. generalist knowledge
- Ability to learn new tools/technologies quickly
- Keeping skills current vs. becoming outdated

### **Influence & Persuasion**
- Ability to convince others without formal authority
- Negotiation skills
- Building consensus
- Presenting ideas compellingly

### **Ethical Judgment & Integrity**
- Handling ethical dilemmas
- Consistency between stated values and actions
- Transparency in communication
- Accountability for mistakes

## Reflection Framework

**For each area, consider:**

1. **Current state**: Where are you now? (Rate 1-10 if helpful)
2. **Evidence**: What specific examples support your assessment?
3. **Trajectory**: Are you improving, stable, or declining in this area?
4. **Impact**: How does this strength/weakness affect your goals?
5. **Development**: What concrete actions could improve weak areas?
6. **Leverage**: How can you maximize your strengths?

---

## Action Planning

After completing your assessment, consider:

- **Top 3 Strengths**: Which strengths should you leverage more?
- **Top 3 Areas for Improvement**: Which weaknesses have the highest impact if improved?
- **Quick Wins**: What small changes could yield immediate benefits?
- **Long-term Development**: What skills require sustained effort over months/years?
- **Support Needed**: What resources, mentorship, or training would help?
